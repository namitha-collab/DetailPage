Professional Learning Sessions

Asynchronous Learning
Math Empowered Core Values (45–60 minutes)
At Math Empowered, we believe mathematical growth comes from engaging in the learning process—not from what students already know. In this session, participants explore the core values that guide Math Empowered classrooms, including fostering a growth mindset, honoring student thinking, and viewing mistakes and productive struggle as the necessary tension that leads to deep understanding.
Asynchronous Learning
Instructional Practices: The Core Four (45–60 minutes)
Explore Math Empowered’s four signature instructional practices: Math Talks, Partner Randomizer Sorts, DARE-ing Application Problems, and Thinking Pathway Games and Tasks. This course introduces the purpose and structure of each routine and demonstrates how they support conceptual understanding, reasoning, and student discourse.
Asynchronous Learning
Unpacking the Kit (15–30 minutes)
Get your BFF Fluency intervention materials ready for immediate classroom use. In this short course, participants learn how to efficiently unpack, organize, and prepare the resources included in the kit.
Live Virtual Session
BFF Multiplication Deep Dive (120 minutes)
Join a Math Empowered team member for an interactive session exploring the mathematics behind the BFF Multiplication Kit and the thinking pathways embedded throughout the materials.
Live Virtual Session
Follow-Up Session (60 minutes)
Reconnect with a Math Empowered facilitator after implementing the BFF Kit in your classroom. Participants reflect on experiences, share successes and challenges, and deepen their understanding of supporting mathematical fluency.
